Charity

Like a lot of people I've taken up Dry January and have been doing it every year since 2018. Rather than just committing to not drinking alcohol I added a twist and decided to do personalised postcards, accepting small donations for each. I created a 'tiered system' for either a postcard or a portrait and it's been a success so far. I have raised around £1200 for cancer research in the years since I started and continue to keep doing it at the start of each year.

Here you'll find a range of what I believe to be my best or most interesting images from across the years so far.
